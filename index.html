Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Read answers to frequently asked questions about booking, operations, diagnostics, and cashless support.

What are the hospital consulting hours?

The Emergency Ward, ICU, and inpatient departments are open 24 hours, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. General OPD and clinical doctor consultation hours occur between 10:00 AM and 8:00 PM daily except Sunday, with doctor schedules varyingly split.

How do I book a direct consultation with a specialist doctor?

You can easily schedule a consultation by clicking 'Book Appointment' on our top bar, selecting the department, choosing your doctor and a preferred date. You can also call us directly at +91 755 264 2960 for booking assistance.

Do you support cashless health insurance plans?

Yes. Kishnani Hospital partners with all major Third-Party Administrators (TPAs) and corporate health insurance carriers in India (including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, ICICI Lombard, Bajaj Allianz, etc.) to provide cashless diagnostic and ward procedures.

Is emergency ambulance pick-up available in Bairagarh and Lalghati?

Yes, we operate a 24/7 dedicated critical ambulance team offering rapid pickup services throughout Bairagarh, Lalghati, Halalpura, and adjacent zones in Bhopal. Please call our emergency response desk at +91 755 264 2960 for immediate help.

What documents are required to claim Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) cashless benefits?

Eligible beneficiaries need to submit their active Ayushman Card (Golden Card), a government-issued photo identity proof (Aadhaar Card preferred), and their family Ration Card at our dedicated reception helpdesk to process cashless clinical admission under the scheme.

Government Healthcare Schemes

We offer standard empanelment under crucial national and state healthcare schemes to provide cashless treatment for eligible citizens of Madhya Pradesh.

  • Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

    Up to ₹5 Lakhs per family per year of free cashless tertiary care hospitalizations for card holders.

  • MP State Government Employee Health Scheme

    Cashless indoor clinical procedures and medical support for active and retired state government officers.

  • Dedicated Helpdesk & Easy Admission

    A specialized desk opposite the main lobby coordinates card validations, documents filing, and fast cashless check-ins.
