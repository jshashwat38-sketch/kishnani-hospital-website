Our History

25 Years of Caring For You & Your Family

At Kishnani Hospital, we believe that health is the foundation of a vibrant, happy life. Since our establishment in 2001, we have grown from a modest outpatient clinic in Bairagarh to a trusted multi-specialty healthcare institution in Bhopal, offering specialized diagnostic rooms, emergency care wards, and sterile surgical suites.

Our vision is to provide high-quality healthcare that is accessible, ethical, and advanced. Guided by our medical advisory board and senior surgical specialists, we focus on delivering personalized treatment plans for each patient, ensuring quick clinical recoveries and minimal stress for their loved ones.

Compassion First

Every patient is unique. We listen attentively and deliver care with deep respect, empathy, and localized warmth.

Clinical Excellence

We combine advanced digital scans, modular operating theatres, and strict hygiene protocols to secure surgical accuracy.

Absolute Trust

Our treatments follow evidence-based medicine and medical ethics, supported by cashless transparency.

Our Journey

Timeline of Milestones & Growth

Follow the structural evolution of Kishnani Hospital from our foundation to our state-of-the-art modern clinics today.

2001

Foundation Stone Laying

Opened as a primary healthcare clinic in Bairagarh, Bhopal, aiming to provide high-quality family medical diagnostics.

2008

Specialty Wards Expansion

Introduced dedicated maternal care, gynecology, and child pediatrics clinics with customized inpatient recovery wards.

2015

Modular Operation Theatres

Built premium sterile surgical rooms and high-fidelity pathology diagnostic labs to conduct internal surgical interventions.

2020

ICU & Critical Care Launch

Established advanced multi-parameter Intensive Care Unit (ICU) wards, digital ventilators, and cashless insurance network tie-ups.

2026

Leading Local Trust Award

Currently recognized as one of Bairagarh's most trusted hospital systems, serving over 50,000 cured outpatient records.
