Insurance Claims

Cashless TPA Admission Guide

Kishnani Hospital partners with all major Third-Party Administrators (TPAs) to settle hospitalization expenses cleanly. Follow this simple process.

1. ID Submission

Provide your TPA Health Card and Aadhaar/PAN ID at our admission desk during check-in.

2. Form Prefill

Our in-house TPA coordinator pre-fills the cashless pre-auth papers detailing your diagnosis.

3. Fast Approval

We securely transmit claims to your insurer for quick, seamless initial credit approvals within hours.

4. Clean Discharge

Discharge smoothly once final medical bills are approved directly by the insurance carrier.
